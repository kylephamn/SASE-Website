About Us

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers was founded to allow Asian professionals to develop professional skills and experiences.

Mission Statement

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) was founded in November 2007 to help Asian heritage scientific and engineering professionals achieve their full potential.

Vision

To empower Asian heritage students and professionals in STEM fields to become leaders in their industries while celebrating their unique identities.

Our Goals

- Provide a platform for networking and mentorship opportunities.
- Encourage personal and professional development through workshops and events.
- Promote diversity and inclusion in STEM fields.
- Foster a community that celebrates Asian heritage and culture.

Our History

SASE was established in 2007 as a national organization aimed at helping Asian heritage students and professionals in STEM succeed in their careers.
